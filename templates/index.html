The begining of a solution better than us


DeasEye was born out of numerous consultations with healthcare professionals, revealing a major problem in the medical field:

The late diagnosis of eye diseases, particularly eye cancers.
Our aim was to fill this gap by providing an effective solution.


Deaseye now offers a clear and fast way for individuals to send x-rays of their retina for early diagnosis of eye conditions, including accurate disease identification.

Our platform thus aims to revolutionize the early detection of eye problems, improving ophthalmic healthcare.
